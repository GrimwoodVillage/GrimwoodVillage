Lay of the Land


Nestled deep within the mountains of San Diego California, Grimwood Village sleeps beneath a veil of mist and myth. Travelers who stumble upon the village speak of its strange beauty and the distant clang of hammers. But most of all, they talk about the shop at the village’s heart, the place where the tattoo artist works his magic.

Ryker isn’t just any inkwright; he’s a craftsman of legends told. His ink is an allegory of rare ingredients, and his hands guided by the spirits of his ancestors. Each tattoo he creates carries a piece of Grimwood itself. Those who wear his work say it changes them: courage finds the fearful, luck follows the lost, and strength wakes in the weary.

People from across the realms venture to find Grimwood. Not just for the art, but to become part of the legend... etched forever in ink and story. After all, not everyone can say they carry a piece of hidden magic on their skin.
